Who am I

Hi! My name is Gabriel Rimoldi Carollo, I am a 21 years old brazilian male living near the city of São Paulo.

I, like most my age, grew up playing video games. So when it came to the point that I had to choose what I was going to do for a living, of course I chose being part of this on growing industry that made my life so good so far.

I've been making games sinse I was 11, when I discovered the rpg maker engine. At the age of 15 I decided to learn something more professional, and take this hobby seriously, so I decided to learn Unity. At the time I was atending a computing course at the same high school I studied, at ETEC Lauro Gomes, and for my final project I made a game called WIDE, wich I unfortunatly lost, that was my first ever "semi-professional" game.

After finishing high school I went to study Digital Games, at FATEC São Caetano Antônio Russo, where I made many games in Unity, developing my skill both with the engine and the c# language. I went on to learn how to make games with native javascript and HTML5 canvas, as well as the Monogame framework. Also dived a bit into web development, project management with Trello and version controll with Git and GitHub.

As of right now, my intensions are to start working in the industry, either by making a game myself or working with a team and finally earn my own money with the hobby that I always loved to do.
